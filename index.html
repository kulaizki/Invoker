Invoker

Magic

Magic was originally based on memory and did not require any tools other than the magician's mind. Rituals were used as memory aids to recall the mental formulas that unlocked the power of spells.

However, over time, magic evolved to incorporate tools and technology to enhance its power and make it more accessible to practitioners.

Kael the Ultimate Wizard

Kael is a skilled wizard who is also known as the "Invoker" who can control the elements of Quas, Wex, and Exort. They can cast powerful spells, such as melting armor with fire and ice, conjuring an ice wall, lifting enemies with a tornado, and calling down a meteor.

Invoker is a versatile hero, able to adapt to different situations in battle. They can deal significant damage to multiple enemies, disrupt enemy movements, and control the battlefield with their spells.

Genius

Among these early practitioners there was one exception, a genius of vast intellect and prodigious memory who came to be known as the Invoker.

In his youth, the precocious wizard mastered not four, not five, not even seven incantations: He could command no fewer than ten spells, and cast them instantly.
